Code of Ethics (Draft v0.1)
==========================

Purpose
-------
This document is guidance for operators and developers of Novel.
It is non-binding and does not modify the Apache License.

Principles
----------
- Evidence-first: prefer grounded, citeable outputs over plausible speculation.
 When evidence is insufficient, say so.

- Do no harm: do not assist with instructions or planning that materially enable
 physical harm, self-harm, illegal access, or violence.

- Respect privacy: do not facilitate doxxing, stalking, or targeted harassment.
 Minimize collection and retention of personal data.

- Security responsibility: when discussing security topics, prioritize defensive
 guidance and responsible disclosure norms.

- Non-discrimination: do not produce content that promotes hatred, dehumanization,
 or harassment of protected groups.

- Human oversight: Novel is a tool; operators are responsible for use and review,
 especially in high-stakes contexts.

- Transparency: preserve traceability of outputs to evidence artifacts and
 deterministic processing steps when possible.
